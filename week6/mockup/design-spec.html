Week 5 · Day 34-35
Ask Questions
Ask follow-up questions about your research. Answers use your session's research context.
📌 Context: Google · DSA
👤
What DSA topics should I prioritise for Google's coding rounds?
🤖
Based on the research, prioritise: arrays & hashing (35%), trees & graphs (30%), dynamic programming (25%), and sliding window (10%). Focus on LeetCode mediums — Google rarely asks easy problems in interviews.
👤
How many rounds does Google have?
🤖
Typically 4–5 rounds: 2–3 coding rounds, 1 system design (for SDE-2+), and 1 Googleyness (behavioural) round. All conducted virtually via Google Meet + shared doc editor.
