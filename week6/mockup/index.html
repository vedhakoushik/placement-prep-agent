Interview Summary
Google's Software Engineer interviews heavily emphasise data structures and algorithms. Expect 4–5 rounds: 2–3 coding rounds (LeetCode medium/hard), 1 system design round for experienced roles, and a Googleyness (behavioural) round. Coding interviews test arrays, graphs, dynamic programming and bit manipulation. Preparation tip: solve 150+ LeetCode problems, focus on optimal time & space complexity, and practise explaining your thought process aloud.
